What do you think?

Exhibitions by nature are mediated experiences. This site represents curated choices that provide a guided tour to what we have learned so far from the data culled from the indenture book. But the project has only just begun and we want to know what you think. This data has many potentials and we couldn't possibly realize all of them, therefore we would like your help to see what we can do to enhance and grow this project. We hope that you'll fill out this brief survey and let us know what you've learned, how your opinions may have changed, and if there is anything you would like to see added. A portion of this information will inform the work of project collaborator, Nicôle Meehan. Fill out the form below to share your thoughts!
